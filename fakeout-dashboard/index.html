Play Console engagement quality

DAU/MAU is the key habit signal. FakeOut is currently low-frequency.

🧭
Interpretation:

A ~5% DAU/MAU ratio means users treat FakeOut as occasional utility, not a daily habit. That is fine for a fact-checking tool, but it makes ads and subscriptions harder unless the app becomes a daily trust feed.

Growth observations

Key user-level patterns from Play Console data.

🇮🇳
India is the biggest opportunity and biggest churn risk.

India appears to be the core market, but growth rates are often negative. Localised hooks and India-specific viral claim coverage matter.

🇺🇸
US had the strongest positive growth spells.

Several positive growth days appeared after mid-March. It is smaller in absolute volume, but worth testing paid/organic positioning.

🚀
Release bumps are temporary.

Versions 81–84 show short-lived improvement. Feature launches need stronger in-app education and external campaigns.

High impact

Turn FakeOut into a daily trust feed

Users do not naturally fact-check every day. Add a lightweight “Today’s viral claims” feed, country filter, and push notifications. This gives users a reason to return without needing to bring their own claim.

High impact

Fix the paywall value proposition

593 paywall views and only 3 purchases means the offer is not compelling enough. Test clearer bundles: unlimited AI checks, video detection, faster processing, saved history, and source reports.

Medium impact

Protect LLM cost before scaling

LLM calls dominate usage. Add caching, duplicate claim detection, rate limits, and cheaper model routing for low-risk queries. Monetisation should be tied to expensive features.

Medium impact

Localise India growth loops

Create India-specific viral claim coverage, WhatsApp-friendly share flows, Hindi/Hinglish summaries, and SEO landing pages for common scams and viral rumours.

Risk

Do not over-index on ads

With low DAU and short sessions, banner ads will likely stay weak. Use ads as secondary revenue, not your main business model.

Next experiment

Run a 14-day retention sprint

Pick one metric: Week-1 return rate or DAU/MAU. Ship one daily content loop, one push notification loop, and one smoother “share to FakeOut” flow. Measure before adding more features.
