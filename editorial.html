About

I'm a Senior Engineering Analyst at Google, working on production ML and AI systems. My work spans analytics, data pipelines, feature engineering, deployment, and LLM governance, including launch readiness, safety reviews, and post-launch monitoring.

Outside of work, I maintain a hands-on homelab: Pi-hole and WireGuard on a Raspberry Pi for network security; a mini-PC running Ubuntu Server with Plex, Nextcloud, Transmission, and Webmin; and a 4070 Ti Super build for local AI experiments with Ollama and OpenWebUI.

Currently building FakeOut: fact checks and AI image detection in a single tap. 11,000+ downloads and counting.

With over a decade of experience in Python and SQL, I focus on building ML systems that actually work in production. I believe in practical solutions, clean implementations, and technology that serves real human needs.
